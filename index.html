About The Author

John E Hudson

John E Hudson served for 6 US Presidents in his 35+ year career and held numerous Senior Leadership positions as a Commanding Officer of 2 Reserve Units and also led a team of over 400 personnel supporting the war fighter in Afghanistan, Iraq and Syria. He enlisted in the US Air Force in 1981 as an Airborne Cryptologic Linguist and served active duty for 5 1/2 years and then another 6 1/2 years in the US Air Force Reserves, separating in 1993. In 1994 he was commissioned in the US Navy as an Ensign in the US Navy Reserves as a Cryptologic Officer (now designated an Information Warfare Officer). He held a Top Secret Security Clearance during his Air Force and Navy careers and received numerous awards throughout his career including a Defense Meritorious Service Award, multiple Navy Commendation Awards and Navy Achievement Awards, multiple Combat Air Medals, and Expert Marksmanship Awards.

About The Book

Afghanistan

A year in picture, Missions and News with the special opertions command

Afghanistan – A Year in Pictures, Missions, & News with the Special Operations Command is a unique perspective on Afghanistan as depicted by John E. Hudson. He was active duty from 2010 to 2013 and selected to be in the Afghanistan Pakistan Hands Program, a Secretary of Defense/ Chairman, Joint Chiefs of Staff initiative that directly supported operations by US Forces – Afghanistan.  As part of his training, he learned various things like language, combat skills, and culture, and was deployed throughout the country with the Special Operations Command. The books comprises his year in Afghanistan with detailed pictures and emails/notes/articles of missions and events that took places in 2011/12
